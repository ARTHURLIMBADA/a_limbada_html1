Our Rooms

Sweet Lemon Boutique Bed & Breakfast offers much more than just crisp sheets, cosy beds and comfortable rooms. Sweet Lemon guesthouse reflects the very same stylish but casual lifestyle that Sea Point has to offer.

We offer the best prices for our rooms, guaranteed. All rooms with private bathrooms are individually postmodern styled and furnished, and measure about 270 - 320 square feet. The tranquil backyard with its cottage and a relaxation area offers a small pool.

The post-modern interior design of this Boutique B&B emphasizes the warmth of the Town House. Nostalgic furniture and equipment are harmonizing perfectly with the modernity of the interior. Every corner shows its own character, which you will hardly find anywhere else.
